AFRICAN CHICKEN GENETIC GAIN(ACGG)

ACGG is an Africa-wide collaboration led by the International Livestock Research Institute (ILRI).

ILRI and partners initiated this new collaboration to provide better chickens to smallholder farmers in Africa.

ACGG tests and makes available high-producing, farmer-preferred genotypes that increase smallholder chicken productivity in Africa.

Live body weight at week 20(LBW) and number of eggs per year per bird(eggpypb)were evaluated on-farm testing. LBW, Epypb, feed intake and survival traits were investigated on-station experiments
